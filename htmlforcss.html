Facts About Japan

Welcome to this simple website about Japan. Here are some interesting facts about the Land of the Rising Sun.

Geography

  • Japan is an archipelago consisting of 6,852 islands.
  • The four largest islands are Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku.
  • Mount Fuji is the highest point in Japan, standing at 3,776 meters.
  • About 73% of Japan is forested, mountainous, and unsuitable for agricultural, industrial, or residential use.

Culture and Society

  • Tokyo is the capital city and the most populous metropolitan area in the world.
  • Japan has one of the highest life expectancies in the world.
  • The country is famous for its unique traditional arts, including tea ceremonies, calligraphy, and flower arranging (ikebana).
  • Modern Japanese culture is globally recognized for anime, manga, and video games.

Economy and Technology

  • The official currency of Japan is the Yen (¥).
  • Japan is a world leader in robotics and automotive manufacturing.
  • The Shinkansen, or bullet train, is famous for its speed and punctuality.

Food

  • Sushi and Ramen are perhaps the most famous Japanese dishes worldwide.
  • Washoku (traditional Japanese cuisine) is registered as a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.
  • Japan has more than 5 million vending machines across the country.
